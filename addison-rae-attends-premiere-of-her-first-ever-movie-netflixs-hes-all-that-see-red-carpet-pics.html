Addison Rae Attends Premiere of Her First-Ever Movie, Netflix's 'He's All That' - See Red Carpet Pic

Publish date: 2024-06-22

Addison Rae‘s first-ever movie is about to be released on Netflix and she just stepped out on the red carpet for the film’s premiere!

The 20-year-old TikTok star and budding actress was joined by co-star Tanner Buchanan at the premiere of He’s All That on Wednesday night (August 25) at NeueHouse in Los Angeles.

Also in attendance were co-stars Madison Pettis, Rachael Leigh Cook, Matthew Lillard, Peyton Meyer, Annie Jacob, Myra Molloy, Vanessa Dubasso, Romel De Silva, Heather Ann Gottlieb, Andrew Matarazzo, Dominic Goodman, and director Mark Waters.

He’s All That is a reimagining of 1999’s She’s All That. In the film, teen social media influencer Padgett’s humiliating on-camera breakup goes viral, leading her to make a risky bet to save her reputation: She swears she can turn scruffy antisocial Cameron into prom king material. But things get complicated when she finds herself falling for him IRL.

FYI: Addison is wearing a vintage Versace dress and Le Silla shoes.
