Why Did Police Short Dead Actress Vanessa Marquez? Explore Her Cause Of Death

Publish date: 2024-04-28

Vanessa Marquez, mostly known as Wendy Goldman from TV show ER, got killed by the police in her own home at South Pasadena, California. Vanessa Marquez is also renowned for her role in drama Stand and Deliver as Ana Delgado.

In 2017, Vanessa Marquez was in the news and in all over the internet for accusing George Clooney, ER co-star, of attempting to blacklist her from Hollywood. Though Marquez frequently shared her health issues on Facebook and Twitter, she still dreamt of winning an Oscar one day. A friend of Marquez named Terence Towles Canote said,

“She was looking forward to life; this is not a woman who wanted to die.”

Mr. Canote also confirmed Vanessa Marquez was having mental health and financial problems at the time of her death.

Vanessa Marquez Cause Of Death

On Thursday morning, LA police officers received a call from Marquez’s landlord; it was reportedly not the first time police help was called out at Marquez’s apartment. When the police arrived, Marquez was having some seizures and she was being uncooperative with LA county mental health clinicians on the phone.

The reason why police shot her was that Vanessa herself firstly brandished a gun at the officers. Nonetheless, the gun she used was a fake one; Lt Joe Mendoza of the Los Angeles County sheriff’s department later said to the reporters,

“It looked like a real gun and it was unclear where the gun was during her lengthy interaction with police.”

The police officers did have body cameras installed in them however the actual footage won’t be released until six months of investigation.

The actress is reported to have mental disorders like depression, OCD, and shopping addiction.
