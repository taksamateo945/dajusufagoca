10 Amazing Things You Can Do On Gumtree South Africa in 2023

Publish date: 2024-06-29

Gumtree is South Africa’s most popular classifieds and advertising platform. It covers listings from the major South African provinces and cities like Cape Town, Durban, Johannesburg, Port Elizabeth, Pretoria, Gauteng, Eastern Cape, Western Cape, KZN just to mention a few. Gumtree South Africa presents you with the unique opportunity to place your classified goods on advert for sale and also to find other important goods to purchase. It is a South African branch of one of the leading classifieds websites known as Gumtree.

Admittedly, the site offers nothing but a convenient and easy way of buying and selling goods such as cars and houses among other things. The good news, however, is that Gumtree South Africa is now available on the Android market to make buying and selling of items even more fun and enjoyable. Now you can be sure that finding that perfect house, the great job, or the pretty pets and cars in South Africa has never been this easy. You may not know this, but there’s more you can find on Gumtree than just the normal items in the grocery store. Scroll down to see the ten amazing things you can find on Gumtree South Africa.

10 Things To Do On Gumtree South Africa

1. Buying and selling sport equipment

For lovers of sports and other awesome leisure activities, Gumtree South Africa is the ideal place. There are unique leisure and fitness equipment, games and a wide variety of sports equipment you can get on Gumtree. Apart from golf clubs, you can also find gyms, treadmills and surfboards. No matter the sports equipment you want, everything is available on the classified page, all you need to do is come online.

2. Buying and selling of new and used classic and modern cars

The website has plenty of amazing cars and they exist in a wide variety. There are good used cars and second-hand types that have all the genuine documents. In addition to this, you can also go for auto parts and accessories for vintage cars.

3. Finding jobs

Finding jobs has always been an uphill task, but at Gumtree South Africa, the exercise is easy. There are jobs and employment classified in South Africa and beyond. Almost all the employment opportunities are placed on this website.

4. Baby items

There’s good news for tired expecting moms, you no longer have to leave the comfort of your cushion to get the best stuff for your baby, on Gumtree, plenty of good deals abound for your baby no matter your budget. There are plenty of kids stuff such as toys, car seats and cots.

5. Events

South Africa is a country with many cultural events that take place throughout the year. You can find popular events such as gatherings, parties and seminars on Gumtree. There are ads that give all the details you need for any event you are interested in attending.

Another fun and exciting thing to do on Gumtree South Africa is to browse through the real estate page. There are lots of exciting information about the houses and apartments. You can make a comparison of the prices of properties either for renting or buying. Any information concerning commercial or residential property for sale is available here.

7. Fashion

For lovers of fashion, Gumtree has all you need in its listings. Apart from vintage clothing, you can find the best ladies wears and shoes.
