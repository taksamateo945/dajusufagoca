Where Are The 1000-Lb Sisters Tammy and Amy Now?

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Tammy and Amy Slaton are currently in Dixon, Kentucky. Tammy, after her weight loss surgery, weighs around 375 pounds and recently lost her husband. Amy, now a single mother of two sons, continues her weight loss journey after separating from her husband.

Season 4 of 1000 Lb sisters chronicled significant changes in the lives of Tammy and Amy. Tammy has returned to Kentucky after completing a weight loss program in Ohio, but she has faced challenges, including the loss of her husband, Caleb Willingham. Meanwhile, Amy has gone through a divorce from her husband, Michael Halterman, while caring for her two sons, Gage and Glenn. The next season of the show will continue to document the personal journeys of Tammy and Amy Slaton as they navigate these obstacles and strive for better health.

Tammy and Amy, sisters who rose to fame on the reality TV program 1000-Lb Sisters, have garnered attention for their unfiltered and often humorous approach to addressing their struggles with obesity. The show chronicles their journey as they confront the challenges associated with severe weight issues, striving to shed pounds and enhance their overall well-being. By offering glimpses into their personal lives, relationships, and the hurdles they encounter along their path to weight loss, the series paints a vivid picture of Amy Slaton Halterman and Tammy Slaton’s lives.

Where Is Tammy from 1000 Lb Sisters Now?

After completing an extensive rehabilitation program for over a year at a rehabilitation facility in Ohio, Tammy is now back in Kentucky. She successfully achieved her target weight. Weighing in at 534 pounds, slightly below her goal of 540 pounds, she was granted approval for weight loss surgery.

Since the operation, Tammy has been sharing stunning photos on her Instagram account, showcasing her remarkable transformation. Now back in Kentucky, she appears to have improved mobility and is rapidly shedding pounds. Recent estimates suggest that Tammy’s current weight is around 375 pounds, as fans discovered she had dropped below the 400-pound mark following her weight loss surgery in February 2023.

Throughout her journey, Tammy faced numerous health crises due to her escalating weight and partying lifestyle. In November 2021, she experienced a life-threatening incident of carbon monoxide poisoning, which necessitated her placement on life support and a tracheostomy. Against all odds, Tammy not only survived but thrived.

During the Season 3 finale in January 2022, Tammy’s brother, Chris Combs, revealed that she had already lost an impressive 115 pounds while undergoing rehabilitation in Ohio after her hospital discharge. The momentous occasion of reaching her target weight, a prerequisite for qualifying for weight loss surgery, was documented in a February 2023 episode of the reality show.

Is Tammy from 1000 Lb Sisters Married?

Tammy got married to Caleb Willingham in November 2022 at the Ohio rehabilitation facility. However, shortly after their separation due to Caleb’s inability to follow his diet, Caleb passed away at the age of 40 on July 1st 2023.

Where Is Amy from 1000 Lb Sisters Now?

Amy from 1000 Lb Sisters’ is currently in Dixon, Kentucky. Following her time on the TLC show, her personal life has also been filled with rapid changes. She experienced marriage, divorce, and the joy of having two children. In 2019, Amy married her high school sweetheart, Michael Halterman. However, their relationship came to an end in March 2023 when Michael filed for divorce.

In response to the divorce announcement, a Kentucky judge decided on March 13, 2023, that the couple would have temporary joint custody of their sons. According to court documents obtained, Michael argued that he deserved joint custody since he had been actively involved in caring for the children since their birth.

While she was married to Michael, they welcomed their first son, Gage, in November 2020, and their second son, Glenn, in July 2022. Despite the separation, Amy has remained focused on her weight loss goals and has also been enjoying her social life as a newly single woman.

Where Do 1000 Lb Sisters Live?

The 1000-lb Sisters, Amy and Tammy Slaton, live in Dixon, a small rural town in Webster County, Kentucky, United States. With a population of around 1,500 people, Dixon serves as the main filming location of the show.

1000-lb Sisters Before and After

Tammy Slaton, one of the sisters featured on the show 1000-lb Sisters, has made significant progress in her weight loss journey. Alongside her sister Amy, Tammy struggled with obesity for many years before seeking medical help. While Amy underwent weight loss surgery in the first season of the show, Tammy faced more challenges due to her higher weight and health risks. It was only after a medical emergency and subsequent treatment in a weight loss rehab facility that Tammy was able to have her own weight loss surgery during the fourth season of the show.

Amy has also experienced remarkable weight loss. Starting at 406 pounds during the first season, she underwent gastric bypass surgery in 2019, resulting in a loss of approximately 130 pounds. Despite concerns about weight gain during her pregnancies, Amy only gained a little over ten pounds and now weighs around 225 pounds, bringing her close to her initial goal.

Tammy faced a longer and more difficult journey compared to Amy. Weighing over 700 pounds during the first season, Tammy struggled to adhere to her doctor’s diet and exercise plan. However, after a medical emergency and a year-long stay in rehab, she achieved her goal weight of 534 pounds and was approved for weight loss surgery. Since then, Tammy has been making remarkable progress and currently weighs around 375 pounds.

Both Tammy and Amy’s weight loss journeys have been inspiring and have improved their overall health and well-being.

Did One of The 1000-lb Sisters Die?

No, none of the 1000-lb sisters is dead. However, Caleb Willingham, who married Tammy Slaton has sadly passed away at the age of 40. His stepmother confirmed the news, but the cause of death was not disclosed. Tammy and Caleb met at a rehab facility in Ohio and got married in 2022. However, they reportedly separated in May, with Tammy filing for divorce due to Caleb not following his diet. Tammy paid tribute to Caleb on Instagram, expressing her love and gratitude.

Additionally, Amy, the sister of Tammy Slaton, shared the heartbreaking news that their beloved dog, Little Bit, had passed away. The revelation occurred during the fourth episode of season 4, where Tammy revealed that their chihuahua had tragically lost its life in April 2022. Amy recounted the heartbreaking moment when she picked up Little Bit from their grandmother’s house, only for the dog to suddenly collapse and stop breathing as they were getting into the car.

Did Amy From 1000 Lb Sisters Divorce?

Amy from 1000 Lb sisters is divorced. In March, Michael, aged 40, initiated divorce proceedings against his former partner, Amy, aged 35. However, prior to that, Amy had accused Michael of domestic violence in February. It is important to note that these charges have since been dismissed.

Based on records from the Kentucky Court, the divorce case between Michael and Amy is still in progress. They were originally set to appear in court on June 1 to address their ongoing divorce and custody issues. However, they have chosen to resolve the matter privately.
