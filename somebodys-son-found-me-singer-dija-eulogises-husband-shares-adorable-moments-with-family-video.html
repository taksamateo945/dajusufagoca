Somebodys son found me, Singer Dija eulogises husband, shares adorable moments with family|

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Nigerian singer and songwriter, Hadiza Blell popularly known as Dija, has taken to social media to eulogise her lovely husband as she shares adorable moments with her family in a video.

KemifilaniNews recalls Dija got married to her lover, Rotimi, in a secret wedding in Kaduna state in 2015. She has always kept her marriage discreet not until recently she started flaunting her kids and husband on the internet.

According to her, somebody’s son (her husband) found her after many attempts of him searching for love and decided to keep it real by getting married to her.

The 32-year-old singer appreciates God for giving her the man of her heart for raising their three kids in a beautiful way so far.

In her words;

Somebody’s Son found me one day and later that day we had 3 Children and added to His Pride! ???‍♀️?

#dtfsundays this passed Sunday was a trip!

3 kids after, God kept me a fresh fish to raise them well. Alhamdullilah

Nigerian stand-up comedian and actor, Okechukwu Anthony Onyegbule popularly known as Okey Bakassi has taken to social media to celebrate his 20th wedding anniversary with his wife, Ezinne.

In a video released, the comic actor marvelled at the growth of their love from how they started the journey of 20 years to date.

Recounting his challenges in marriage, he revealed there were times nobody believed in their love, but they stood to fight for it. Also, starting off with his wife and children as a middle-class citizen in a BQ and having to stay apart due to the nature of his job were not so easy, but they pulled through.
