Kentucky Flooding (2022) - Legacy.com

Publish date: 2024-05-19

At least 15 people have died in flooding in eastern Kentucky. Many others are missing, and rescue and recovery efforts are underway.

The names of the deceased have not yet been released to the public.

This is a developing story. As more information becomes available, we will update this page. For full story and ongoing coverage, please visit the Lexington Herald Leader.
