Publish date: 2024-04-09

Terry Crews and Jimmy Fallon are making perfect shirtless harmony. The Brooklyn Nine-Nine actor, 45, stopped by The Tonight Show to promote his new movie Blended, and showed off one of his hidden talents. 

While he arrived at the late-night talk show in a camo-printed suit, Crews soon stripped down, going shirtless for a duet with Fallon, 39. The pair sang a rendition of Paul McCartney and Stevie Wonder‘s 1982 song “Ebony and Ivory,” while flexing their pectoral muscles to “nip sync” along to the tune.

The action star showed off his cut chest, while Fallon was seemingly forced to opt for a chest double as his head can be seen superimposed over a buff, oiled-up hunk’s torso.
