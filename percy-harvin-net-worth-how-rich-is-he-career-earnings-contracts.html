Percy Harvin Net Worth: How Rich Is He? Career Earnings &amp; Contracts

Publish date: 2024-03-20

Percy Harvin is an American former professional football player. He played eight seasons with four different clubs throughout his NFL career, earning $42.21 million in total. The Minnesota Vikings selected him in the first round of the 2009 NFL Draft. Harvin’s career also featured stops with the Seattle Seahawks, New York Jets, and Buffalo Bills.

Harvin was instrumental in the Seahawks’ Super Bowl triumph against the Broncos in his first season with the organization. Following that, in November 2014, Seattle dealt the wide receiver to the Jets. Following a brief stint with the Jets, Harvin signed a one-year deal with the Bills in 2015. In 2016, he returned for one more season hampered by injury before retiring from professional football.
