Steve Aoki and Lauren Jauregui team up for new single 'All Night'

Publish date: 2024-02-29

DJ and producer Steve Aoki has been teasing his collaboration with Fifth Harmony songstress Lauren Jauregui for a while now, but the wait is finally over.

‘All Night’ dropped on Friday and it was certainly worth the wait. The sultry offering showcases Steve’s slick production skills whilst Lauren’s soulful vocals glide effortlessly over the hypnotic beat.

You got me paralyzed, and I think I like it
Caught me by surprise, I’m not usually like this, no
Got me paralyzed, don’t think I can help it
Why’s it feel so right?
Let’s keep this going all night

Speaking about their collaboration in a statement, Steve said: “I’m such a fan of Lauren and her incredible voice. Working together was a no-brainer for me – she is crazy talented and I’m so proud of this song.”

Lauren added, “This song was so special to work on, perfecting and vocal producing, and writing. So grateful to Steve for trusting me and my process.”

Take a listen to ‘All Night’ below.

The track follows on from Steve’s album Kolony, which was released earlier this year. It has also been revealed that he will be releasing a remix of BTS’ ‘MIC Drop’ on November 24. A teaser was recently released, which you can listen to here.

‘All Night’ is Lauren’s latest solo endeavour. Having recently featured on Ty Dolla $ign’s ‘In Your Phone’, she has also previously collaborated with Halsey and Marian Hill. Additionally, she continues to deliver incredible songs with Fifth Harmony, who recently released ‘Por Favor’ with Pitbull and ‘Can You See’.

‘All Night’ is available now.
