Valentine’s Day is upon us! A day of love, chocolate and flowers — and, more importantly, an excuse to wear a super cute outfit. That’s what the holidays are truly about after all, no?

Your Valentine’s Day plans this year might be just staying home, ordering in and watching a movie, but that doesn’t mean you can’t get into the spirit of the holiday with a themed piece. This sweater, for example, nails it — and it looks equally cute with sweatpants or a skirt!

This pull-on sweater is not your typical cozy, chunky winter sweater. It operates more as a top than a layering piece. It’s still super soft and comfy though, so you get the best of both worlds here. This piece has a loose fit with a truly gorgeous drape. We love the way the three-quarter sleeves fall nonchalantly over the arms, dipping down from the dropped shoulder seams. And that wide V-neckline? Perfect for letting a shoulder show every now and again!

Of course, the standout features of this sweater is the big heart design in front, solidifying it as a romantic Valentine’s Day choice. Don’t feel as though you need to save it solely for February 14 though. This piece is easily versatile enough for other occasions too, whether they be other holidays or simply a trip to the mall!

This heart sweater is currently available in four colors: grey with a white heart, beige with a red heart, red with a black heart, or white with a black heart. Which one you buy is up to you — plus, we are seriously having trouble choosing a favorite and don’t need that kind of responsibility in our lives!

When you’re heading out for a day on the town, try tucking just the front of this sweater into a pair of jeans, or even letting it hang loosely over some simple leggings.
