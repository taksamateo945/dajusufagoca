Nia Vardalos, Shirtless John Corbett Do My Big Fat Greek Wedding 2 Pic

Publish date: 2024-01-29

Don’t forget the Windex!

Nia Vardalos and John Corbett are back together once more! The actors stopped to take a photo while on the set of My Big Fat Greek Wedding 2 on Friday, June 26. And, oh, the Sex and the City alum kept his shirt off for the special occasion.

“Is someone trying to get baptized again in #MyBigFatGreekWedding2?” Vardalos, 52, teased via Instagram. In the pic, the costars looked giddy as they got ready to film in the Portokalos family restaurant.

Vardalos and the Parenthood alum, 54, played Toula Portokalos and Ian Miller, respectively, in the 2002 flick My Big Fat Greek Wedding. It was announced in May 2014 that a sequel was on the way.

“Now that I’m experiencing motherhood, I’m ready to write the next chapter of my family story,” Vardalos said in a statement at the time. She joked: “Of course, a few jaded folks in the press corps will claim I ran out of money or just want to kiss John Corbett again. One of these things is true.”

My Big Fat Greek Wedding 2 hits theaters on March 25, 2016.
