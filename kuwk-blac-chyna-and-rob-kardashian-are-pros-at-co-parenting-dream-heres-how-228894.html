KUWK: Blac Chyna And Rob Kardashian Are Pros At Co-Parenting Dream Heres How!

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Despite the fact that they were not able to make their relationship work out, when it comes to co-parenting their baby girl Dream, Blac Chyna and Rob Kardashian are pros! One insider shared with HollywoodLife why the exes work together so well!

Apparently, the two know to put their child first no matter what!

‘The one thing Blac and Rob have figured out is co-parenting. Everything else is a bit of an issue, but anything that benefits Dream is both on Blac and Rob’s priority list, and they’ve easily figured out how to manage their lives around Dream and what’s best for her. What was best for Dream will always win. Blac and Rob are always on the same page for her,’ the source shared with the news outlet.

As fans of KUWK know, Rob spent Christmas with Dream and the rest of the Kardashian-Jenner clan this year and another insider revealed how he and Chyna agreed on that arrangement.

They explained that ‘It took a lot of back and forth with Blac Chyna, it wasn’t easy for Rob to get Dream for Christmas. But, it happened, and Rob and his family are grateful to Blac Chyna for that one thing at least this holiday season, because it made Rob so happy. The whole family loved having Dream there, they love her so much. But, the biggest gift of all for them was seeing Rob so happy and so full of energy. Kris was elated.’

The whole Kardashian Christmas party was very heartwarming since all the parents, including the new ones (Khloe and Kylie), were accompanied by their young ones.

That being said, in a video posted by Khloe on Instagram, even Rob made a rare appearance on camera.
