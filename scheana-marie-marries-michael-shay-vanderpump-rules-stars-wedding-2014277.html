Scheana Marie Marries Michael Shay: Vanderpump Rules Star's Wedding

Publish date: 2024-07-07

Pop the champagne! Vanderpump Rules star Scheana Marie tied the knot with her fiance Michael Shay at the Hummingbird Nest Ranch in Santa Susana, Calif., on Sunday, July 27, Us Weekly can confirm.

The bride and groom were feted by several close family and friends, including former Real Housewives of Orange County's Gretchen Rossi and Slade Smiley, Golnesa "GG" Gharachedaghi and Leila Gharache of Shahs of Sunset, and Big Brother couple Rachel and Brendon Villegas. Former Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Joyce Giraud and Lisa Vanderpump were also in attendance. 

Hours before their nuptials, Scheana and Shay took to Twitter to share their excitement about saying "I do." "Omg!!!! @MikeShayMusic 0 days 21 hours 54 mins! U still down to do this!?" Scheana wrote on Saturday. Shay replied: "So excited to marry my best friend tmrw! I love you sooo much sweetheart!"
