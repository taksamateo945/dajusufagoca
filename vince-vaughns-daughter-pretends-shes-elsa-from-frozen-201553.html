Vince Vaughn's Daughter Pretends She's Elsa From Frozen

Publish date: 2024-02-10

He has a little ice ice baby on his hands! Vince Vaughn sat down with Ellen DeGeneres on Thursday, Feb. 5, where he revealed that his daughter has an obsession with Frozen‘s Elsa, like many of her peers including Blue Ivy Carter.

The father of two, 44, told DeGeneres that his oldest child Lochlyn, now 4, is obsessed with Disney’s animated hit “like it is her job!”

“I like Frozen. I think Frozen is great,” the Unfinished Business actor noted. “But it’s interesting to me because of the two ladies in the movie. One is all in with the first guy she meets, right?” he said of Princess Anna. “She’s just: ‘Here’s my heart, here’s everything,’ and then, you know, he kind of turns out not to be the best of choices.”

Little Lochlyn, though, has come to “idolize” Anna’s big sister, Snow Queen Elsa. “She doesn’t want to get too close to anyone, because she’ll hurt them — she’ll freeze them and she’ll cause them pain so she has to shut herself out,” Vaughn said of Elsa’s characteristics. “I wonder what a child psychologist would say about this, but my daughter puts on the white wig and then she is Elsa… She says, ‘I’m shutting you out, I’m not good for you. I have to be far, far away.'”

Vaughn speculated that a “little role-playing” and “imagination” were good for kids, but he wondered whether he and his wife Kyla Weber were “doing some serious damage” with their tot. “She’ll freeze me and then I’ll go along with it, so maybe I’m encouraging it,” he mused. “I’ll freeze, but then she’ll kiss me and unfreeze me. And so then I say to her: ‘You don’t have to shut me out because if you do freeze me you’ll kiss me to unfreeze me.'”

While Lochlyn has been mimicking Elsa’s frigid mannerisms, she’s actually been incredibly warm to her baby brother Vernon, 18 months. “She’s enjoying it. She’s very sweet and nurturing,” Vaughn said of his daughter. “Sharing can be a problem sometimes, but for the most part she’s really excited.”
