Mzansi spirit: 4 Viral videos prove South African schools are flames

Publish date: 2024-01-27

South Africans had goosebumps over videos of different local high schools showing off their spirit.

War cries and hip-hop renditions - South African schools remain undefeated and we decided to take a look at four videos that captured the school spirit in Mzansi.

1. Hilton Boys

Caster Semenya shared a video of Boys Of Hilton's 'war-cry' and it gave social media users the feels.

"Spirit! #BoysOfHilton @siyaxButhelezi @boysofhilton," she captioned the post.

2. St David’s Marist Inanda

Previously, We reported a video went viral which showed the St David’s Marist Inanda's rendition of Fat Joe's All The Way Up.

The video showed the all-boys school's spirit during assembly and it gave Mzansi the feels - it even got recognised on an international scale when Fat Joe shared it on his Instagram account.

"MAMA AFRICAAAA CAN YOU HEAR MEEEEEEEE. We all the way upppp," he captioned the video.

3. St Stithians College

Another high school war cry has made its way online. St Stithians College held nothing back and their video quickly went viral, gathering more than 278 000 views since it was shared online on Saturday.

4. Queen's College

Social media user Aphiwe Xanywa shared a video of her little brother leading the gwijo at Queen's college.

The video was viewed over 99 200 times on Twitter.
