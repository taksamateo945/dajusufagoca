Justin Bieber Giving Refunds For Cancelled M&amp;G

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Justin Bieber’s Purpose tour will be giving out partial or full refunds to anyone who purchased the Ultimate VIP Experience ,and  I’ll Show You VIP Experience.

Justin made a public announcement on Instagram that he will be cancelling all meet and greets due to the constant need of not being the expectation people led him to be. He commented, “I end up feeling so drained and filled with so much of other people’s spiritual energy that I end up so drained and unhappy…” 

Beliebers appreciated his honesty, but those who paid to get the experience of a life time, weren’t so canny of hearing this news.  Looks like there is a partial solution for all.

Partial Refunds will be returned to those who kept the ticket. For the Ultimate VIP Experience, fans will be getting $500.00 U.S. dollars. The  I’ll Show You VIP Experience, fans will be receiving $375.00 U.S dollars.

The partial refunds will work, for only those who kept the ticket, and still want to attend the concert with the seats they purchased. There is an option to get a full complete refund, but that will mean that you can no longer attend the show. WAUN VIP packages will not be affected.

The Purpose VIP Team have also said that they will no longer accommodate the VIP package elements; VIP packages contained merchandise, backstage tour, and etc. This is only for the North American leg, and European VIP are also cancelled.

The Biebs has kicked off the Purpose tour on March 9. 2016, and will be preforming on the IHeartRadio Festival this Sunday, April 3rd. Other artist like Maroon 5, Meghan Trainer, Fetty Wap, Zayn Malik, DNCE and etc. will share the same stage. He surely is making a big comeback!
