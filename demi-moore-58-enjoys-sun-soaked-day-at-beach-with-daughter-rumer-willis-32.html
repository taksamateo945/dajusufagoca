Demi Moore, 58, enjoys sun-soaked day at beach with daughter Rumer Willis, 32

Publish date: 2024-08-04

DEMI Moore gets in the spirit with her eldest daughter on holiday.

The Ghost actress, 58, joined Rumer Willis, 32, for a sun-soaked day at the beach in Mykonos, Greece.

Her youngest daughters Scout, 29, and Tallulah, 27, are also on the family getaway.

The actress appeared in her first campaign she’s done with her three daughters last month.

The Striptease actress, 58, Rumer, Scout, and Tallulah, launched “Together” with fashion swim brand Andie.

The collection wants to celebrate time spent with families.

Andie Founder and boss Melanie Travis said: “They all look hot in all these suits, right?

“The styles are designed to be flattering across age demographics, across body sizes.”

The costumes are in the “most-loved” styles costing from £68 to £83.
