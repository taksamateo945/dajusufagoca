Chopra revealed what keeps her motivated to stay in shape to You & I. "Instagram inspires me," she admits. "You'll see dozens of posts every day, where people flaunt their toned bodies, their fitness regime, and the food they eat. Chopra tells Times Of India that she likes intermittent fasting as a way to stay healthy. "I think I've taken to intermittent fasting like a fish to water," she says. Chopra revealed her favorite form of exercise to Times of India. "Suryanamaskar is my go-to exercise. It relieves me of all the stress, especially the in the back. The asana keeps the spine in perfect condition and the nervous system on point."

Chopra opened up about her diet to You & I. She says that she pays attention to what goes in her body. "I remember when I shifted to Mumbai; I was not much into fitness, but then when I went to the gym I simultaneously tried fad diets. I can say that they don't work!
