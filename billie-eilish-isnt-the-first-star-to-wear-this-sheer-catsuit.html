Billie Eilish isnt the first star to wear this sheer catsuit

Publish date: 2024-01-29

It’s official: Summer’s hottest trend is see-through catsuits.

British Vogue’s June 2021 issue stars Billie Eilish as we’ve never seen her before, dressed in sexy lingerie-inspired looks including a custom, curve-hugging Mugler jumpsuit.

For the pinup-inspired shoot, the 19-year-old singing sensation modeled the sheer mesh onesie with a corset and strappy heels from the same fashion house.

“I love these pictures and i loved doing this shoot. do whatever you want whenever you want,” Eilish, who typically favors oversized outfits, captioned the jaw-dropping photo on her Instagram. “F—k everything else.”

In fact, the pop star is just the latest in a slew of stars to sport the daring design. In 2019, Bella Hadid strutted down Mugler’s runway in a near-identical catsuit, which was later also worn by Dua Lipa (who happens to be dating Bella’s brother, Anwar Hadid).

The “Levitating” singer might just be Mugler’s biggest fan; she’s also sported versions of the revealing look in the pages of Rolling Stone, on stage and more.

Eilish isn’t even the first pop star to wear Mugler’s eye-popping catsuit in the pages of British Vogue; Beyoncé showed off her shape in the cutout creation on one of three October 2020 covers of the magazine.

Miley Cyrus, meanwhile, chose a very similar Mugler catsuit for the 2020 iHeart Radio Festival, and both Kylie Jenner and Irina Shayk put their own spins on the latest iteration from the brand just last month.

The only rule when it comes to rocking this delicate design? Look, but don’t touch.
